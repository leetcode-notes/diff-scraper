    I'm Looking For Help

    Buyer's Agents: Buyer’s agents represent the buyer, not the seller. Their primary role is to provide market insight, research and suggest listings, assist with negotiations, organize all legal documents and represent the buyer’s interests throughout the home purchase process.

    Seller's Agents: Seller's agents represent the seller, not the buyer. Their primary role is to list the home for sale, market it to potential buyers, organize all legal documents and disclosures, and represent the seller's interests throughout the sale process.
