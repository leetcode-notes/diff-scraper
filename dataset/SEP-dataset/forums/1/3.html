  1. Raggy Administrator Staff Member

    Dear friends,

    As some of you may have noticed, we started sending out a new batch of invites last week. Our aforementioned issues have been resolved and we’re very glad to announce that invites will (finally) roll out in larger batches from here on out!

    In August we have completed the tier 1 members roll out. If you’ve joined our contests and was there at the pop-up store, you should have received an invite by now.

    In September our main focus is on our early supporters, active forum members and the reservation list. The amount of people in tier 2 (early supporters and active forum members) is huge, so it might take a while to receive your invite. It is amazing to see that we’ve grown into such a big community in a short amount of time.

    We will also start rolling out shareable invites this month. After receiving your invite and purchasing your OnePlus 2, shareable invites will be available short after (we’re talking weeks rather than months this time). When exactly you will receive your shareable invite depends on the level of stock. Word of mouth is the reason we’re here, and we hope you can our shared love of great tech with your friends and family.

    Remember to keep an eye on your email inbox and add *@oneplus.net to your email whitelist. You may find a nice surprise in your inbox soon!
     
    Raggy, Sep 7, 2015
