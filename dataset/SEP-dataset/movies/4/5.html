The Green Inferno (2014)

Watch Trailer

Cast: Lorenza Izzo, Ariel Levy, Aaron Burns, Daryl Sabara, Kirby Bliss Blanton, Magda Apanowicz, Sky Ferreira

Director: Eli Roth

Rating: R

Running Time: 103 min.

Synopsis: Eli Roth returns to the director's chair with this ode to Ruggero Deodato's notorious film, Cannibal Holocaust, with this Worldview Entertainment production surrounding a group of plane crash victims with run into some blood-thirsty natives in the jungles of Peru. Roth co-wrote the script with his Aftershock collaborator Guillermo Amoedo.
