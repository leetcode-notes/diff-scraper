About the Trending Now Badge

Trending Now companies fall into the top 15% of
Simply Hired's Employer Brand Index™.

Simply Hired’s EBI is defined as the frequency with which job seekers engage with a company’s jobs. Higher engagement acts as an indicator of greater job seeker preference and a stronger employer brand. Simply Hired calculates its EBI daily to give you the most up-to-date and relevant trending companies.
